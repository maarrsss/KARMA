Valentine Valmesia is the Founding Vampire and strongest vampire in existence. He was created directly from **Ultidea**'s starlight and **Ulgerra**'s vana. He is the Chief Caretaker of **Ember's End**, as well as the Fourth Seat of the **Karmic Lords**.
