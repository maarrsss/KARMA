Rue, full name Rusomenanthis, is the second Sattva created by Ultidea at the Dawn of Creation. He was originally in charge of the Sacred Watch, the strongest military division in the Heavenspointe, but after the first invasion by the **Hyperians**, he left and took his army with him to settle on the Karmic World and join the **Karmic Lords**, where he resides in the **Kingdom of Ossica**.
