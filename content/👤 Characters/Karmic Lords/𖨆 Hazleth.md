Hazleth, also known as Hazleth of the Earth, is the Mother of All Giants and the third member of the **Karmic Lords**. She was created by **Archegonia** using his cells, **Earth Sigils**, and **Ultidea**'s **starlight**. 

# APPEARANCE

Hazleth has the appearance of a very beautiful woman with four arms,  medium-length blonde hair, sapphire blue eyes, and battle-worn tan skin. When in casual attire, she wears  a black outfit that features a stylish fur-like collar. They have accessorized with a necklace that showcases a circular pendant with an intricate design.

# PERSONALITY

At first glance, Hazleth seems very cold, distant, and unapproachable. She puts up this demeanor because, in her own words, she is too nice and can't say no to any request. She has a very bubbly and outgoing personality, in stark contrast to what people usually think she is like.
