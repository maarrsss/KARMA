Euphrexia is the Queen of the Elves and Chief Gardener of the **Aspersus**, as well as the Fifth Seat of the **Karmic Lords**.

### PERSONALITY

Euphrexia has a calm demeanor, much like her friend [[𖨆 Michael]], but hers is much more strict and reserved. This strictness comes from the fact that she is Ulterra's perfect copy, so she also has her inherited laziness, which she doesn't like.
