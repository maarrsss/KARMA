Mariaposa von Magica is the **Queen of the Faeries**, and was created by **Ultidea** himself by mixing starlight into all sigil types. She was tasked by Ultidea to oversee the ecosystem of the world. She resides in the floating city of Maegaerium.

### APPEARANCE

Mariaposa takes the appearance of a beautiful young woman with long, hot-pink hair, blue eyes with a pink swirl design in the iris, and pale skin. She wears a a long, elegant, pink gown with a flowing design. It has a white inner layer with a deep V-neckline and a fitted bodice. Over this, there is a sheer, light pink outer layer that adds a soft and dreamy look. The outer layer has long, wide sleeves and a flowing skirt that opens in the front to reveal the white inner layer. The waist is accentuated with a light pink embroidered belt.

### PERSONALITY

Because of her revered status as **Queen of the Faeries**, she puts on a very elegant and soft personality in front of others. But when she is comfortable, her real bratty and harsh, but still very kind and loving personality shows. Also due to her immense crush on Archegonia, she tends to act like a lovestruck teenager around him.

She is best friends with [[𖨆 Euphrexia]], with whom she enjoys private tea parties with alongside [[𖨆 Valmesia]].
