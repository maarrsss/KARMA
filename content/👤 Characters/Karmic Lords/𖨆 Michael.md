Michaelangelus is the second Ulvikar created by Ultidea at the Dawn of Creation. He left his post as Governor as the Eighth Circle of the Hellespointe and Chief Commander of the Armies to join the **Karmic Lords** as the Second Seat.

### APPEARANCE

Michael, in human form, has the appearance of a tall and handsome young man with blonde hair, demonic red eyes, and pale skin. He hides his horns so no one will know of he is an Ulvikar, and even though he is the ruler of all Dark Elves, he does not have pointy ears.

### PERSONALITY

Michael is a very laid-back and relaxed person, rarely raising his voice or even showing any sort of extreme emotion like anger or happiness. He is an extremely hard person to get irritated. 

Moreover, he is also very friendly and kind, a personality not common among vikars. Because of this, he gets along very well with Hazleth to the point where she considers him her 'big brother'
