Veil is an undercover faery that was sent by Mariaposa 50 years ago to keep tabs on the growing political power of the School of Dharma. He uncovered Mitella and Othary's betrayal, and was almost killed by both of them; surviving this only because of the help of [[𖨆 Sylverwulf]] and [[𖨆 Yuru]].
