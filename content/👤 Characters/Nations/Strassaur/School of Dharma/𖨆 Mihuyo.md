Mihuyo Yaminazek is a Goliath from the Kulles Khanagate who gained fame throughout Galia for her strength and purity, eventually joining the School of Dharma as a Teacher. After 5 years, she would begin to feel disgust for the institution, especially their prejudice towards non-humans. 

She would not fight against Yuru, instead joining his side, but she would fight against [[𖨆 Othary]] and nearly best her, but was stopped from dealing the killing blow from [[𖨆 Nyterro]].
