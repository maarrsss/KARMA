Ghallan Gutinavo is the Eight Teachers of Dharma in the School of Dharma, teaching Right Intention. Although aware of the schemes cooked by the traitors within the school, his family being kept hostage forced him to carry out their plans. 

Due to grief and extreme isolation, he lets [[𖨆 Sil]] almost kill him, but because of Sil's natural ability to read the body, she knew what was going on with him and spared his life. 

He would defect and join Yuru in stopping the invasion.
