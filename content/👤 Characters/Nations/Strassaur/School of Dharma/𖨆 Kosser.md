Kosser Majarne is one the Eight Teachers of the School of Dharma, teaching the Right View. He fought against [[𖨆 Sylverwulf]] and [[𖨆 Traighan]], beating Traighan but losing to Sylverwulf. He would later defect after suspecting betrayal from within.
