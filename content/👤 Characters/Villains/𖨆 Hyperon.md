Hyperon VI House Hyscalathon is the emperor of the Hyperian Empire and a central antagonist in the ***KARMA*** series. He is the first variant accidentally created by [[𖨆 Hyperus]]

# PERSONALITY

Hyperon is an elegant, charming, and manipulative man. Because of his immense power, he developed a god complex and sees himself above any other lifeform (excluding the Vedas). 

That said, he loves his people. So much so that he enslaved millions of versions of himself just so none of his people would ever see hard labor.
