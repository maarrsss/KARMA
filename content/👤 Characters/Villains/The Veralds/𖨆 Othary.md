Othary II Vitello is a noblewoman from the Kingdom of Quderis who joined the ranks of the School of Dharma as a Counselor so she could spy on the Strassaun royal family. She had no interest in either side, only participating in the invasion so she could confirm the dead Teachers. 

She is part of the organization known as The Veralds.
