Unity is the Verald "Unity". He was born from the corpse of a dead woman. He lived his life as a being with no name, no purpose, no friends or family. He saw war, hate, famine, and death. He saw how humans would kill other humans. This disgusted him, and he would grow to become a vigilante. He would become famous throughout the Western nations as a one-man army due his ability to create parallel existences of himself. 

He would eventually meet [[𖨆 Yuphranes]] and join her as a Verald.
