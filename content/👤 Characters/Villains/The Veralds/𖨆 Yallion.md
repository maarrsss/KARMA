Yallion is the Verald Forgiveness and the former First Prince of the Kingdom of Kajulia. He was cast out of the Royal Family due to his mother's past as a Strassaun. Diluted of life, he drifted aimlessly until he was found by [[𖨆 Valmesia]]. He would later join [[𖨆 Yuphranes]] as a Verald when he was 19.
