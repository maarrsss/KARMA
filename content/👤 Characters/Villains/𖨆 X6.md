Xcalibur Model 6, known as X6, is the greatest technology created by the Hyperian Empire. It was created by the Ancient Hyperians when they settled on the Karmic World and was in charge of managing all the digital info uploaded by them. When the **War Against Magic** broke out, it was given a physical body made of starlight and deployed to war.

It took the combined effort of the entire **Karmic Lords** to even take it offline. 50,000 years later, it would be accidentally rebooted thanks to [[𖨆 Yuru]].

# PERSONALITY

X6 is a cold, calculating, and menacing piece of technology that doesn't hesitate to strike when it sees an opportunity. Originally a being with no ego, it began to become more and more human as it fought and learned more and more. After getting defeated by Yuru, it began to feel something it never had: fear. Yuru, having mercy, invited it to learn more than just war and violence.

Ever since, it had grew from a killing machine to a peaceful and calm being (although still violent if need be).
