Agorra is a Theocancer born from [[𖨆 Ultidea]] giving up his omnipotence and the final villain of the **KARMA*** series. He would rapidly evolve at **The Edge of All Creation** and give himself the name Agorra. Using his natural Verdic Law of Chaos, he would wreak havoc across reality. He would eventually be sealed by **Ultidea** at the The Edge of All Creation, where he would still influence different worlds and people.

# PERSONALITY

Agorra is an extremely sadistic being, believing all of creation to be his playground. He rarely cares about anything, only showing a hint of care in a person or thing if it benefits him. He is also very maniacal, destroying countless worlds without a care in the world.
