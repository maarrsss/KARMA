Atreus is arguably one of the most powerful of the Vikan Lords, even if his status doesn't show it. He is also one with the worst temper, often times snapping at his little brother [[𖨆 Theyestes]]. He is ultimately killed by Theyestes and his vikarma is absorbed by him using a forbidden technique taught to him by a mysterious man named The Magician.
