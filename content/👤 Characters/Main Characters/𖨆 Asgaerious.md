Asgaerious the Old, or his full name known as Asgaerious Ulantino, is the oldest Beastman alive. He was born 200 years after the Days of Unity, and saw the most brutal times of war. He is singlehandedly responsible for the population of [[Vikanthropes]] going down from an estimated 500,000 to only 40,000. He was recruited to the Beast Captains hundreds of years before [[𖨆 Sylverwulf]] and the others.
