Hyperus Hyascalathon is the accidental creator of [[𖨆 Hyperon]] and every other offshoot. He was born on the planet **Hyperia** and accidentally consumed a large amount of [[𖨆 Ultidea]] 's omnipotence, but because his mind couldn't handle the divine power, he accidentally split himself into two and created [[𖨆 Hyperon]]. Hyperon would then try and kill him, leading Hyperus to run away to different worlds and dimensions to escape. 

### PERSONALITY

Due to his appearance, he is often mistaken for a female even though he is biologically a male. It also doesn't help that he very caring and soft-spoken, loves to cook and bake, and also likes cute animals. He doesn't like violence or has any motivation to fight, but will do it if it means his survival or the survival of others.

He doesn't even hate [[𖨆 Hyperon]], as he sees him as his fault because of his inability to handle **starlight**.
