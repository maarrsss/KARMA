Yuru is the main protagonist of **KARMA*** and the reincarnation of **Ezra Martin**.

# PERSONALITY

Yuru is a complex individual. As a very logical and analytical person, he operates on a less emotional and more pragmatic and necessity-based logic. He is extremely smart and tactical, often being compared to a great general. This outward composure, however, often serves as carefully constructed **armor**, shielding the deep trauma and emotional complexities stemming from his past life. Yuru possesses remarkable **self-awareness**, understanding both his analytical tendencies and the underlying emotional drivers that often contradict his stoic front. Beneath his logic resides powerful **empathy** for the suffering and fierce **loyalty** towards his chosen allies, driving him to protective extremes.

Despite his sharp intellect granting keen insights into others' motivations, he struggles significantly with social graces, often appearing **blunt or distant** due to ingrained mistrust and lack of conventional experience. When confronted by those he deems enemies, his pragmatism takes a darker turn; unable to kill directly due to his spiritual nature, he utilizes calculated, often brutal **non-lethal methods** designed to inflict maximum suffering, sometimes exhibiting a **chilling satisfaction** in enacting this 'justice'. This intricate personality is laced with resilience, creativity, and a dry, sarcastic wit used to cope with his reality.

# APPEARANCE

As a Spirit, he is just a floating ball of light not much bigger than a baseball. As he evolves, he becomes much bigger. At his final evolution as a Veda-Theosophicore, he becomes a big as a car tire and gains four pairs of translucent wings.

In human form, he looks like a feminine-androgynous human with long pure-white hair, deathly pale skin, and big silver eyes that have no pupils. He is fairly tall, standing at 5 feet and 11 inches.
