Sylverwulf, her real and full name being Sylverwulf III House Sylvanus, is the current **King of the Lycans**. She serves as the First Head of the **Karmic League** as one of its founders, along with [[𖨆 Yuru]].

### PERSONALITY

Sylverwulf is a very free-spirited and charismatic individual. often defying conventions and embracing her own path. Her charisma makes her a natural leader, and she can easily rally others to her cause. She has a playful and adventurous side, enjoying the thrill of exploration and discovery. Despite her regal bearing, she is approachable and empathetic, always willing to listen to her people's concerns and is always ready for battle.
