Chronoriel is the God of Time and part of the Firmament Sisters, alongside [[𖨆 Ouraniel]] and [[𖨆 Hadesriel]]. 

She was very good friends with [[𖨆 Hyperus]] , often keeping him company and enjoying human life. After [[𖨆 Hyperon]]'s betrayal, she was the one that alerted [[𖨆 Ultidea]], who then saved Hyperus's life. Unfortunately, Chronoriel would be trapped within Hyperon's heart.
