Ouraniel is the Goddess of Space and a Firmament Sister. She oversees the nature of space, along with her sister Chronoriel, to make sure Ultidea's creation is maintained. 

After the First Hyperian War, she would create the nation Ixuria and seal off the entire city of Ixur within a separate dimension to hide , with only a dome visible from the outside.
