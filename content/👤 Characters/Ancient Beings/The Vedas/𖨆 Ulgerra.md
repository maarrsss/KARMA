Ulgerra, also known as the Dragon Mother, is the Veda twin sister of [[𖨆 Ulterra]], the little sister of [[𖨆 Ultidea]] and mother of all dragons. She tasked herself in charge of overseeing the power balance of the world is not tipped too unfairly on one side.

### PERSONALITY

Ulgerra is a very stern, calm, and reserved person. Her sheer aura commands absolute respect in any room she walks in on. However, beneath her calm exterior lies a deep well of compassion and a fierce protectiveness for her children—the dragons—and the world she guards. She values wisdom and knowledge, and she is always willing to share her insights with those who seek her counsel. Despite her stern demeanor, she has a soft spot for the innocent and the vulnerable, and she will go to great lengths to ensure their safety.
