Ultidea is the Supreme God and author of creation, as well as the architect of the Karmic World. He is the big brother to **Ulgerra** and **Ulterra**, father and creator of the **Ulvikars** and **Ulsattvas**, and creator of many other characters.

### PERSONALITY

Ultidea, despite being an omnipotent god, had the personality more of a reserved and graceful nobleman that enjoyed a good story. He loved playing jokes as well, being known as a jokester to people that knew him.
