 Ulterra is the Veda twin sister of [[𖨆 Ulgerra]], Veda little sister of [[𖨆 Ultidea]], Goddess of the Elves, and creator of the Aspersus. She resides in the Asper Dimension where she overlooks the entire Galian continent.

### PERSONALITY

Ulterra is a very laid-back and lazy individual, to the point where she created a perfect replica of herself to manage her kingdom. She resides in the Asper Dimension because she watches over the Galian continent like a unlimited TV program. She loves humanity, not in a pet kind of way, but she sees them as her own children. She knows every single induvial humans' name, date of birth, favorite food; she knows everything about every single person.

Before the **War Against Magic**, she was very involved in the lives of humans and was seen as a goddess. After the first invasion by the Hyperian Empire, she locked herself in the Asper Dimension to love humanity from afar.
