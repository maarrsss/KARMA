Ymir is the Titan Ancestor and the strongest Titan in existence. He was created from Archegonia's cells, [[𖨆 Ultidea]] 's starlight, and Sky Sigils. He fought off the Hyperians, and sacrificed himself to seal the Hyperians within the Firmament.


### **APPEARANCE**

While in Titan form, Ymir's face is that of a 700 foot tall, metalloid being golden trims, sapphires, and other rare metals. His eyes have void-black sclera and golden irises. 

Even in human form, Ymir is still towers over most people. He stands at 234 cm (7'6 feet) and is extremely muscular. His brown skin, short, light-brown hair, and golden eyes make him extremely attractive to mortal women. Due to possessing **starlight**, Ymir has a tattoo under his eye of four stars of varying sizes.


### **PERSONALITY** 

Ymir is generally a very gruff but laid back individual. In his own words, he doesn't make time for the weak. To even be considered worthy to take up his time, you must survive a punch from him. Despite this, Ymir still cares for those he deems weaker than him, seeing as how he sacrificed himself to keep the Hyperians trapped in an endless time loop in the Firmament and lost his mind.
