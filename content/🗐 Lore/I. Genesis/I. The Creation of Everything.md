The Null, a quasi-existence, dominated all. 

From it emerged the Ultimate Idea, the First Veda, who called itself [[𖨆 Ultidea]]. [[𖨆 Ultidea]] reversed the Null in on itself to create reality. [[𖨆 Ultidea]] wielded [[Starlight]] which is the ultimate creation power. Bored, Ultidea would spawn and create countless universes to satiate it's boredom. 

From these worlds, [[𖨆 Ultidea]] 's two twin sisters would be born: [[𖨆 Ulgerra]] and [[𖨆 Ulterra]]. Ulgerra's Veridic Law was Deconstruction/Decay, while Ulterra's Veridic Law was Construction/Growth. 

From his own Veridic Law of Potentiality, [[𖨆 Ultidea]] would establish **karmic energy**. He would create the Samsaara, a means of which life and souls could be remade in different lives depending on one's karma. 

For the souls of those who led evil, he created the **Hellespointe**, a place where one's soul would be tortured for eternity if they do not seek redemption and learn from their mistakes. For the virtuous, he would create the **Heavenspointe** where one can rest their soul for eternity. In exchange, their karmic energy is used to create a new soul.

To oversee these two realms, [[𖨆 Ultidea]] himself would make 14 beings, mixing them with his own [[Starlight]] energy and karmic energy. These would be the 7 **Ulsattvas**, in charge of the Heavenspointe, and the 7 **Ulvikars**, in charge of the Hellespointe.

After this, he would get rid of his omniscience so that he can properly enjoy his creation.
