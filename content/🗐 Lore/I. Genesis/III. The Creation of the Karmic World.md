#### THE GENESIS

[[𖨆 Ultidea]] would return to the original realm he awoke in and carefully created the Karmic World. After that, he would give up his omnipotence in fragmented pieces. A large chunk of it would be taken and managed by Samsaara, another big chunk would be found and absorbed by [[𖨆 Hyperus]] and created [[𖨆 Hyperon]], while the rest would be the fuel to endlessly spawn creation, as well as give birth to [[𖨆 Agorra]].

Now, all [[𖨆 Ultidea]] had left was his immortality.

He would then task [[𖨆 Ulterra]] with maintaining life in the Karmic World, and tasked [[𖨆 Ulgerra]] with overseeing the power balance.

[[𖨆 Ultidea]] would create a being named [[𖨆 Archegonia]], who would be a blueprint for all races. A perfect and immortal being, he would be the progenitor for all major races (except the **Dragons** and **Elves**). [[𖨆 Ultidea]] would task [[𖨆 Archegonia]] with creating other races from it's cells.

 [[𖨆 Ultidea]] would also create [[𖨆 Mariaposa]] and the rest of the faery race to watch over the ecosystem of the world. [[𖨆 Mariaposa]] would create and establish the Maegaerium to oversee this task.

To go on with her mission of maintaining life, [[𖨆 Ulterra]] would set up the [[Kingdom of Aspersa]], creating the Elves as assistants to the mission. The first elf ever made would be a perfect clone of [[𖨆 Ulterra]] mixed in with [[𖨆 Ultidea]] 's starlight energy named [[𖨆 Euphrexia]].

[[𖨆 Archegonia]] 's crusade would last a total of 10 years, and in that time, he would create races like Titans, Giants, Dwarves, and countless other races. The last 3 races he created, his masterpieces he referred to as the Trichoracia, would the Lycanthropes, Humans, & Vampires.

[[𖨆 Ultidea]]  absolutely loved humans; a species that didn't have any intrinsic abilities, were durable, had innate karmic energy, and could grow stronger. He liked them so much that he spread the essence of humanity all over the omniverse.
