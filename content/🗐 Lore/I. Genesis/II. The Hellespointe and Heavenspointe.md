### **HELLESPOINTE**

The Ulvikars would begin to carve their territories, some fighting amongst each other (*Onthens vs. Buddakai*) while others would just join forces (Vita and Brittany). Latear, in charge of the entire realm, didn't need to fight.

Because the Hellespointe was created entirely from vikarma, it naturally gave birth to billions of vikars. They would begin to fight amongst themselves, slaying each other and creating chaos. Millions of vikars would challenge the Ulvikars, none succeeding. 

Latear would organize a tournament, with the 8 strongest vikars being named Princes and managing an Ulvikar's territory. Since time doesn't exist in the Helles, billions of hypothetical years went by as vikars slew each other and got stronger. Then, the 8 strongest would appear.
