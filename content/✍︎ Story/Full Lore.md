# GENESIS
In the beginning was the Vull, a quasi-existence of non-existence. That is to say, nothing existed. Emerging from this was the first Veda, [[𖨆 Ultidea]], with his Veridic Law being [[Starlight]]. His birth would start creation and blanket the Vull under a layer of existence and creation.

Emerging from creation was [[𖨆 Ulgerra]], Veda of Deconstruction with her Veridic Law being [[Vana]], and [[𖨆 Ulterra]], Veda of Construction and her Veridic Law being [[Mana]]. The three Vedas would come to see each other as siblings, creating a platonic and familial bond, with Ultidea acting like a aloof big brother.

# CREATION OF EVERYTHING

Ultidea, using [[Starlight]], would create the entire cosmology of KARMA. He'd create the Samsara and establish the order of life and death, creating three sisters to uphold the fabric of reality: [[𖨆 Ouraniel]] God of Space, [[𖨆 Chronoriel]] God of Time, and [[𖨆 Hadesriel]] God of Death. They would be called the Sisters of the Firmament, or The Firmaments. From their essence, they would form the **Ides**, an army of beings born from their essence. 

Alongside them, Ultidea would create the Samsara as a means for life to always exist. If one could reach Nirvana, they would be free of the Samsara and become immortal. He would create two realms: Naraka ([[The Hellespointe]]) where souls of the wicked were sent to contemplate in suffering, and the [[The Heavenspointe]] where one could rest eternally with the option to re-enter Samsara for a chance to attain enlightenment.

To oversee the realms, he would create the 16 Primordials using a mix of [[Starlight]] karmic energy: [[𖨆 Latear]] and the Ulvikars by mixing his starlight with [[Vikarma]], Umile and the Ulsattvas using [[Dharma]].

After this, Ultidea would give up his omnipotence and omniscience to enjoy his creation fully. He would travel through countless worlds, enjoying life on each one.

# THE HEAVENLY WAR ON ENTROPY

A large part of Ultidea's omniscience and omnipotence would be absorbed by the Samsara, giving it a very weak ego. However, the omnipotence and omniscience not absorbed began to coalesce and give birth to the Fourth Veda:

[[𖨆 Agorra]], the Divine Cancer. With the Veridic Law of Chaos he caused havoc across reality. He targeted the Samsara so no more life could exist across the omniverse and to generally cause as much chaos as possible.
