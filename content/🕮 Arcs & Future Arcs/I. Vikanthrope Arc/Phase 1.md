18-year old Ezra Martin dies in a house fire while on a sleepover with his best friend, [[𖨆 Tsagi Li]], and little sister. The last thing he sees before he dies is Tsagi smiling at him. 

He then wakes up in a pitch-black void, where the voice of the Samsara evaluating his karma. It says "error" over and over again and tells Ezra that he has the perfect balance of karma, so he is unable to go to Heaven or Hell. It would continue to try and send him to either realms, saying "error" over and over again. It would do this for a hypothetically billions of years, with Ezra's soul being [[dharma]] and [[vikarma]] and overloaded with huge amounts of raw [[Karma| karmic energy]]. 


# REINCARNATION

Ezra would wake up in the Blackheart Forest, where he would meet [[𖨆 Sylverwulf]]. After a brief back and forth, Sylverwulf would ask for his name. Because he is an anomaly and the Samsara tried to wipe what it could, he did not know his previous name, so Sylverwulf gave him the name [[𖨆 Yuru]]. 

Sylverwulf, who was on a mission before she met Yuru, would take him as a pet. Not seeing any better options, Yuru agreed. Sylverwulf would inform him that she had been tracking monsters called [[Vikanthropes]], which are cursed Beastmen from centuries ago that destroy whatever they see. A battle ensues between Sylverwulf and a pack of Vikanthropes in which Sylverwulf is easily victorious, highlighting her immense skill and power.

However, they would quickly be overrun by Vikanthropes and surrounded. In the nick of time, [[𖨆 Traighan]] and [[𖨆 Asgaerious]] would come in to check up on Sylverwulf. After introducing himself to the two new strangers, Yuru would be taken to the Old Outpost to meet with the other Beast Captains and discuss the situation at hand.
