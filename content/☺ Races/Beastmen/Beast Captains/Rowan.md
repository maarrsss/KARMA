

Rowan Hitaguri is the 8th Beast Captain of the Beastman Army, and holds the title of **Analyst**. He becomes good friends with Yuru during the events of the Lycanthrope War.

##### **Appearance**

Throughout the series, he has been called feminine by most people and even mistaken for a girl. 

Rowan is of medium height with long, dark green hair, warm golden skin, and bright golden eyes. He often wears a black fur coat, sporting a dark, oversized shirt with a large, stylized yellow bird design on the front. The bird's wings are spread out, and the tips of the wings have a pinkish hue. Beneath the shirt, he had a green, patterned garment that's visible at the collar. Below, he wore dark pants with a belt.

###### **Beast Form**
Rowan's Beast Transformation turns him into a large green panther with glowing, golden eyes, two tails, and a strange triangular symbol on his back.

##### **Personality**

Rowan, at first, is a very shy and reserved individual, preferring to be indulged his studies, books, and being in nature in private than be with other people. This makes him very bad at basic social interactions, to the point where he can barely even explain his plans to the other Beast Captains. He also insists on staying as the 8th Beast Captain due to his fear of military responsibility and managing the lives of others
