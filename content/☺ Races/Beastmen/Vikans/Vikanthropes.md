> # A common Vikanthrope
> ![[Vikanthrope.jpg]]


A Vikanthrope is the uncontrolled and forced vikarmic evolution of a Beastman. The lesser and more weaker ones are instinct-driven and have no real intelligence, however, the stronger a Beastman was prior to evolution, the smarter.

Below is the racial hierarchy, top being strongest, bottom being weakest:

- **VIKAN KING**: A Vikan King is a Beastman who possessed the King's Factor of the Beasts prior to vikanization. The absolute strongest form of the sub-ace

- **VIKAN LORD**: Intelligent Vikanthropes capable of speech, reasoning, and high intellect. However, they are still extremely evil and corrupt.

- **VIKAN GENERAL**: Weaker than a Vikan Lord, still capable of intelligence and reasoning

- **VIKAN COMMANDER**: Way weaker than a Vikan General, but a bit dumber lol

- **COMMON VIKANTHROPE**: Irrational, dumb, and always listens to the most powerful Vikanthrope around.
