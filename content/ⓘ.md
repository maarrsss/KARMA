While on a sleepover with his best friend and little sister, 18-year old Ezra Martin dies in a house fire. Amazingly, he wakes up and hears a voice telling him he has a perfect balance of karma, so he is unable to go to Heaven or Hell. As a last resort, he is reincarnated into the Karmic World as a Spirit named [[𖨆 Yuru]]. 

Now on a mission to find his best friend, Yuru befriends numerous monsters, gods, and heroes. His new life is fraught with action, adventure, and hidden secrets.

# WHAT ARE ALL THE ARCS?

All arcs of the story can be found in the **Arcs & Future Arcs** section of the wiki. In total, there are seven arcs (making for a total of about 43 planned Light Novels and 300+ chapters). Here are all the planned arcs.

• [[✎ Vikanthrope Arc]] 
The first arc of the series, this arc starts off with Ezra's death and reincarnation into the Karmic World as a Spirit. On a quest to find his best friend, he allies with the Beastmen and helps them fight against a centuries-old curse.

• [[✎ School of War]]
The second arc of the series, this arc focuses on the School of Dharma's invasion of the newly reconstructed Beastman kingdom of Regnum Animale. This arc focuses on introducing the politics of the world, introducing new characters, and setting up future locations.

• [[✎ Race to the Seal]]
The third arc of the series, this arc focuses on showcasing the Karmic World, as well as also introducing new important characters and organizations.

• [[✎ War of Heaven and Earth]]
The fourth arc of the series, this showcases the power and strength of characters and also setting up new layers of the power system, as well as setting up big plot twists and reveals.

• [[✎ The Ancient Thesis]]
The fifth arc of the series, this is the stepping stone to the endgame arcs of the series. Introduces new powers, abilities, and reveals lore secrets.

• [[✎ Hyperia]]
The sixth arc of the series, this reveals all hidden lore and secrets, as well as tests the power of all characters since the [[✎ War of Heaven and Earth]].

• [[✎ Entropy's End]]
The seventh and final arc of the series, this is the final battle of the series and concludes the story of ***KARMA***.
